Logo Refinement

LK Design & Build

Blueprint grid concept evolved. Warm off-white on black, refined sans-serif tagline, no box or dimension lines.

Design direction: Keep the LK letterforms and blueprint grid — remove the containing box and all dimension lines/arrows. Tagline set in Inter (refined sans-serif) with increased letter spacing. Colour shifted from cold gold to warm off-white on black. Six variations explore weight, grid density, spacing, and tone.

A — Clean Grid

Full 5x5 grid visible behind LK. Inter Light at 0.45em spacing. Warm off-white #F0EAE0 on near-black. Direct, readable, balanced.

Full grid Light 300 #F0EAE0

B — Separator Line

Full grid with a subtle horizontal separator between LK and tagline. Tagline smaller (16px) with wider 0.55em spacing and reduced opacity. Slightly warmer #EDE6D8.

Separator Wide spacing #EDE6D8

C — Ultra Light

Extra Light weight (200) on both LK and tagline. Larger 180px letters. Ghost-like grid at 5% opacity. True black background. Most minimal and premium feeling.

Extra Light 200 True black #F5EFE3

D — Major/Minor Grid

Centre cross uses slightly heavier stroke (major grid) while other lines are minor. Accent underline beneath LK. Adds subtle architectural hierarchy to the grid.

Grid hierarchy Accent line #E8E0D0

E — Ghost Grid

Barely-there grid at 4% opacity. Tagline at 15px with 0.6em spacing and 65% opacity — very understated. Lightest warm white #FAF6F0 for maximum contrast on deep black.

Ghost grid Max spacing #FAF6F0

F — Double-Density Grid

Primary grid plus finer sub-grid between lines — richer blueprint texture. Extended grid span beyond the letters. Tagline at 60% opacity for a layered depth effect.

Sub-grid Extended span #F0EAE0

Compared to Original

Original — Gold, box, dimension lines
Refined — Warm off-white, no box, sans-serif tagline
