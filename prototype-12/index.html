Our Story

LK Design and Build was founded on a simple belief: that great design should be accessible, and quality construction should be the standard, not the exception.

Led by Lyda, our team brings together interior design expertise with hands-on building knowledge. This dual perspective means every design decision is grounded in buildability, and every construction choice is guided by design intent.

From initial concept through to final handover, we manage every detail. Our clients across Adelaide trust us to deliver spaces that are not only beautiful, but purposefully designed for how they actually live.

Our Process

A clear, collaborative journey from first conversation to final handover. No surprises, just purposeful progress.

Step 01

Discover

We begin with a conversation. Understanding your lifestyle, aspirations, and the practical needs that will shape every decision. This is where listening becomes design intelligence.

Step 02

Design

Concepts take shape through spatial planning, material palettes, and 3D visualisations. We refine together until every element feels right and every detail serves a purpose.

Step 03

Refine

Detailed documentation, material specifications, and comprehensive pricing. Full transparency on costs, timelines, and scope so you move forward with complete confidence.

Step 04

Build

Quality construction managed with care. Regular updates, site cleanliness, and respect for your home. Our integrated team ensures the design intent carries through every stage of the build.

Step 05

Deliver

The final reveal. A space that exceeds expectations, with styling guidance and aftercare support. This is not just a handover — it is the beginning of how you will live in your new space.
