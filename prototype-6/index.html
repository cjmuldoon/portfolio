About Us

Crafting Adelaide’s most beautiful homes

At LK Design and Build, we believe your home should be a sanctuary—a place that reflects who you are and how you live. Led by Lyda, we bring together exceptional interior design with expert building craft to deliver spaces that are as functional as they are beautiful.

From coastal retreats in Semaphore to character home renovations in Unley, every project receives our full creative attention and meticulous building standards. We handle everything from first sketch to final handover, so you can enjoy the journey.
