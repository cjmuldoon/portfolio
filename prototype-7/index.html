Services

What We Build

It doesn't matter which room you're looking to make over — we'd love to help. Our job is to listen, suggest ideas and then work with you to find the perfect renovation solution.

Bathrooms & Kitchens

The spaces you use most, designed to feel beautiful every single day. From full gut-and-rebuild bathroom renovations to custom kitchen design with premium joinery, stone benchtops, and integrated appliances. We handle tiling, waterproofing, plumbing, electrical — every trade, managed under one roof.

  • Custom cabinetry and joinery
  • Tile selection and installation
  • Waterproofing and plumbing coordination
  • Lighting design and electrical

Major Renovations

Whether you're renovating to sell or renovating to stay, we transform entire homes. Open up floor plans, modernise living areas, add natural light, and create flow between indoor and outdoor spaces. We manage council approvals, engineering, and every stage of the build so you don't have to.

  • Full home makeovers and reconfiguration
  • Structural modifications and open-plan conversions
  • Council and planning approvals
  • Renovate-to-sell packages

Additions & Extensions

Love your location but need more space? We design and build seamless extensions that feel like they were always part of the original home. Second storeys, rear extensions, alfresco areas, granny flats — all engineered to match your existing structure and exceed current building standards.

  • Second-storey additions
  • Rear and side extensions
  • Outdoor living and alfresco areas
  • Granny flats and ancillary dwellings

New Builds

Ground-up construction with uncompromising quality. We work with you from initial concept through to handover, managing every stage of the design and build process. Your dream home, engineered to last generations, with the personal attention only a boutique builder can provide.

  • Custom architectural homes
  • Full design-and-build packages
  • Site assessment and feasibility
  • Fixed-price contracts with no surprises
