Elevation Lines Identity System

Version 1.0 — March 2026

01

Logo

The LK Elevation Lines mark frames the "LK" letterforms between two horizontal datum lines with tick marks at each end, referencing architectural elevation drawings and height markers. A dashed centreline anchors the composition vertically. The wide landscape format and clean geometric lines communicate precision, openness, and measured confidence.

Gold on dark — primary
Cream on charcoal

On light background

Charcoal on cream
Gold on white

Minimum Size

To preserve the legibility of the elevation lines, tick marks, and centreline, the logo must never be reproduced below these minimum sizes. The landscape format requires extra width to read correctly.

40px — Icon mark minimum
120px — Full logo minimum

Clear Space

Maintain a minimum clear space equal to 1x the height of the LK monogram on all sides of the logo. The wide landscape format means the horizontal clear space must be measured from the tick mark endpoints, not the letterforms.

04

Brand Voice

The LK brand voice strikes a balance between professional authority and genuine approachability. We speak with the confidence of expertise without alienating our audience.

Premium

We communicate quality through restraint. Our language is refined, never ostentatious. Every word earns its place, just as every design element earns its position.

Precise

Technical accuracy is non-negotiable. We use specific language, real measurements, and concrete details. Vague claims are replaced with demonstrable facts.

Confident

We state what we do clearly and directly. No hedging, no over-promising. The work speaks, and our words match its quality — nothing more, nothing less.

05

Logo Misuse

To protect the integrity of the LK identity, the following modifications are strictly prohibited. The logo must always appear exactly as specified in these guidelines.

Do not stretch, squash, or distort the logo proportions. The landscape format and line spacing are precisely calibrated. Always scale uniformly.

Do not rotate the logo. The elevation lines must always remain perfectly horizontal.

Do not recolour the logo outside the approved palette. No gradients, no rainbow effects, no unapproved tints.

Do not add drop shadows, glows, bevels, outlines, 3D effects, or any other visual treatments to the logo.

Do not place the logo on busy photographs or patterned backgrounds that reduce legibility of the fine elevation lines and tick marks.

Do not alter the spacing between the LK monogram, the datum lines, the tick marks, the centreline, or the "DESIGN AND BUILD" tagline.
