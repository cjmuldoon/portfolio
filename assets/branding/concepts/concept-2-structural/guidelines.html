Structural Frame Identity System

Version 1.0 — March 2026

01

Logo

The LK Structural Frame mark constructs the "LK" letterforms from bold I-beam cross-sections — flanges, webs, and structural joints. A horizontal datum line runs through the junction, grounding the composition. With no enclosing shape, the open format conveys strength and transparency, referencing the steel frameworks at the heart of modern construction.

Gold on dark — primary
Cream on charcoal

On light background

Charcoal on cream
Gold on white

Minimum Size

To preserve the legibility of the I-beam structural details and flange proportions, the logo must never be reproduced below these minimum sizes.

40px — Icon mark minimum
120px — Full logo minimum

Clear Space

Maintain a minimum clear space equal to 1x the height of the LK monogram on all sides of the logo. Despite the open format with no enclosing shape, the datum line and letterforms define the spatial boundary.

04

Brand Voice

The LK brand voice strikes a balance between professional authority and genuine approachability. We speak with the confidence of expertise without alienating our audience.

Premium

We communicate quality through restraint. Our language is refined, never ostentatious. Every word earns its place, just as every design element earns its position.

Precise

Technical accuracy is non-negotiable. We use specific language, real measurements, and concrete details. Vague claims are replaced with demonstrable facts.

Confident

We state what we do clearly and directly. No hedging, no over-promising. The work speaks, and our words match its quality — nothing more, nothing less.

05

Logo Misuse

To protect the integrity of the LK identity, the following modifications are strictly prohibited. The logo must always appear exactly as specified in these guidelines.

Do not stretch, squash, or distort the logo proportions. The I-beam structures must maintain their engineered ratios. Always scale uniformly.

Do not rotate the logo. It must always appear level and upright with the datum line horizontal.

Do not recolour the logo outside the approved palette. No gradients, no rainbow effects, no unapproved tints.

Do not add drop shadows, glows, bevels, outlines, 3D effects, or any other visual treatments to the logo.

Do not place the logo on busy photographs or patterned backgrounds that reduce legibility of the structural details.

Do not alter the spacing between the I-beam letterforms, the datum line, or the "DESIGN AND BUILD" tagline.
