Brand Guidelines

Cornerstone Identity System

Version 1.0 — March 2026

01

Logo

The LK Cornerstone mark places carved serif "LK" letterforms within a solid charcoal parallelogram block with a chamfered top-right corner. A gold accent line anchors the base. The solid fill shape, stone-like texture, and serif typography evoke permanence, craftsmanship, and the foundational cornerstone of a building — a mark of lasting quality.

Gold on dark — primary
Cream on charcoal

On light background

Charcoal on cream

Minimum Size

To preserve the legibility of the carved serif letterforms and the chamfered corner detail, the logo must never be reproduced below these minimum sizes.

40px — Icon mark minimum
120px — Full logo minimum

Clear Space

Maintain a minimum clear space equal to 1x the height of the LK monogram on all sides of the logo. The solid block shape defines the primary boundary, with the tagline extending below.

1x 1x 1x 1x

04

Brand Voice

The LK brand voice strikes a balance between professional authority and genuine approachability. We speak with the confidence of expertise without alienating our audience.

Premium

We communicate quality through restraint. Our language is refined, never ostentatious. Every word earns its place, just as every design element earns its position.

Precise

Technical accuracy is non-negotiable. We use specific language, real measurements, and concrete details. Vague claims are replaced with demonstrable facts.

Confident

We state what we do clearly and directly. No hedging, no over-promising. The work speaks, and our words match its quality — nothing more, nothing less.

05

Logo Misuse

To protect the integrity of the LK identity, the following modifications are strictly prohibited. The logo must always appear exactly as specified in these guidelines.

Do not stretch, squash, or distort the logo proportions. The parallelogram block and chamfered corner must maintain their exact angles. Always scale uniformly.

Do not rotate the logo. It must always appear level with the gold base line horizontal.

Do not recolour the logo outside the approved palette. The charcoal-and-gold relationship is essential to the Cornerstone identity. No gradients or unapproved tints.

Do not add drop shadows, glows, bevels, outlines, 3D effects, or any other visual treatments to the logo beyond the specified carved effect.

Do not place the logo on busy photographs or patterned backgrounds that reduce legibility of the carved letterforms.

Do not alter the spacing between the LK monogram, the parallelogram block, the gold base line, or the "DESIGN AND BUILD" tagline.
