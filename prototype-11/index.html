About LK Design and Build

Crafting spaces that inspire

At LK Design and Build, we believe your home should be a reflection of who you are. Led by Lyda, our Adelaide-based studio brings together interior design expertise and construction craftsmanship to deliver spaces that are as functional as they are beautiful.

From concept to completion, we manage every detail. Whether it's a contemporary renovation, a sensitive heritage restoration, or a complete new build, we approach every project with the same uncompromising commitment to quality and design integrity.

What We Do

Our Services

Interior Design

Bespoke interiors that balance aesthetics with the way you actually live. Material palettes, spatial planning, and curated finishes.

Renovations

Transforming existing homes with considered design and expert construction. Kitchens, bathrooms, living spaces, and full-home renovations.

Additions

Seamless extensions that feel like they were always part of your home. Second storeys, rear extensions, and studio additions.

New Builds

From the ground up, designed and built as one seamless process. Your vision, our expertise, a home without compromise.
