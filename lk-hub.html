Brand Guidelines Summary

Brand Voice

Premium but approachable. Confident without arrogance. We speak to homeowners who value quality craftsmanship and thoughtful design. Warm, knowledgeable, trustworthy.

Logo Usage

Minimum clear space: 1x the height of the LK monogram on all sides. Never stretch, rotate, or recolour the logo outside the approved palette. Use icon mark below 40px.

Photography

Warm natural lighting. Show spaces in use — lived-in, not sterile. Emphasise materials, textures, and craftsmanship details. Avoid heavy filters or oversaturation.
