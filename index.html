About

I build tools that bridge the gap between complex systems and the people who use them. From AI-powered classification engines to automated scheduling bots, my work centres on making technology invisible — so users can focus on what matters.

My projects span full-stack web applications, data pipelines, ML integrations, and creative front-end experiences. I'm drawn to problems where automation can eliminate tedious manual processes, and where a well-designed interface can turn raw data into actionable insight.

When I'm not coding, I'm probably at the gym (where I've automated my booking and workout tracking), or monitoring how much my dog barks while I'm out (also automated — he's a certified good boy).
