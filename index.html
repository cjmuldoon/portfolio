About

I build SaaS platforms and tools that bridge the gap between complex systems and the people who use them. With over 15 years in enterprise operations — from facilities coordination through to business systems leadership at Ventia — I've seen firsthand how the right automation can transform the way teams work.

My projects span full-stack web platforms, cross-platform mobile and desktop apps, AI/LLM integrations, data pipelines, and NLP classification engines. I'm drawn to problems where automation can eliminate tedious manual processes, and where a well-designed interface can turn raw data into actionable insight.

When I'm not coding, I'm probably at the gym (where I've automated my booking and workout tracking), or monitoring how much my dog barks while I'm out (also automated — he's a certified good boy).
