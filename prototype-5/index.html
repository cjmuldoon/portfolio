About Us

Where Vision
Meets Craft

LK Design and Build is an Adelaide-based design and construction studio led by Lyda, specialising in creating spaces that balance beauty with everyday functionality. We bring a considered, detail-driven approach to every project.

From complete home renovations and additions to interior styling and bespoke new builds, we partner with our clients from initial concept through to final handover, ensuring every decision reflects your lifestyle and aspirations.
