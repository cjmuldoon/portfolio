Our Story

We believe your home should feel like a warm hug

Prime Design & Built started with a simple idea: renovations should be exciting, not stressful. We're a team of designers and builders who genuinely love what we do -- and it shows in every space we create.

Based right here in Adelaide, we understand the unique character of South Australian homes. From heritage cottages in Unley to coastal retreats in Semaphore, we bring warmth, craftsmanship, and a personal touch to every project.
