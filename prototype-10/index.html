// About LK Design and Build

Where engineering
precision meets
design intuition

LK Design and Build is a premium Adelaide-based practice specialising in residential renovations, additions, new builds and bespoke interior design. Led by Lyda, every project is approached with the rigour of an engineer and the eye of a designer.

We believe your home should be a precise expression of how you live — not a compromise. From initial concept through to final handover, our integrated design-and-build approach eliminates the gaps between vision and execution.

Every detail is considered. Every material is intentional. Every space is calibrated to your life. This is what precision by design means.
